PRIVACY POLICY

effective July 16, 2026

The short version: HIVEMIND has no accounts, no analytics, no ads, no tracking cookies, and no game server. Your save data never leaves your browser. We don't want your data — we want you to keep bees.

What the game stores

Your game progress (the state of your colony, your upgrades, and settings such as the sound preference) is saved with your browser's localStorage, on your own device. It is never transmitted to us or anyone else. Clearing your browser data for this site deletes it permanently.

What we collect

Nothing, directly. This site runs no analytics scripts, sets no cookies, and has no sign-up, comment, or contact forms.

Third-party services

Serving the site involves three infrastructure providers, each of which may process your IP address and standard request metadata (browser type, pages requested, timestamps) as part of ordinary web hosting:

  • Netlify hosts the site and keeps standard server logs. See the Netlify privacy policy.
  • Cloudflare provides DNS for hive-mind-game.com. See the Cloudflare privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts serves the two typefaces the game uses; your browser requests the font files from Google's servers. See the Google privacy policy.

We do not receive, combine, or analyze any of this data ourselves.

Children

HIVEMIND is suitable for all ages and collects no personal information from anyone, children included.

Changes

If this policy ever changes — for example, if the game gains an online feature — the new policy will be posted here with an updated effective date before the change takes effect.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue at github.com/kmay89/hivemind/issues.
